2025 U.S.–China Rhodes Scholar Dialogue

Agenda & Registration

Event Overview

This dialogue is initiated, convened, and hosted by Rhodes Scholars from China. Bringing together globally renowned scholars, policy practitioners, business leaders, and cultural observers—most of whom are Rhodes Scholars or alumni—the series explores evolving U.S.–China relations across Geopolitics, Economics & Technology, and Society & Culture. As the 10th anniversary of the Rhodes Scholarship in China approaches, the event offers rigorous, respectful, and forward-looking conversations.
